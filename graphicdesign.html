The Musicals - Alphabetical Poster

Design Concept

The design concept of this poster is to make an introductory poster for popular musicals. The musicals are selected from the list of most popular Broadway musicals and the Wikipedia. Because different musical posters have different sizes and scales, it would look somewhat messy if I put all the posters on one page without processing them. Therefore, I used a heavy weight typeface to be the contour of each musical poster. I also applied a filter to lower the brightness of each musical poster, such that the color of each alphabet character would not stand out and fade into the background.
