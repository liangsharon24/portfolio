Business Dynamic and Library

Data manipulation to discover hidden links.

Project Overview

This is an individual project that utilized the tool of data manipulation to explore the relationship between density of public library and the indices of business dynamic. The data was obtained from various database and websites. Results showed that the indices of business dynamic and the density of public library has a mild negative correlation.

Project Motivation

Library is considered as a place that provides free resources of knowledge to every person regardless of their background or social status. However, whether the public libraries has a positive effect on the economy of a certain area is still undiscovered. In this project, I explored the relationship between the amount of library and the economic mobility in different states.

Part B. Correlation

I correlated the job-increasing index and the area covered by a single library in a given states by Pearson correlation (the larger the area covered, the lower the density). The result showed a weak positive correlation (r = 0.198). This suggests that in the areas with higher job creation rates than the job destruction rates tend to have a lower density of public libraries. However, the correlation is not significant, which suggests smaller possibility of direct relationship between these two indices. There might be some other factors are affecting the business dynamic.
