Project Overview

Reef is a smart thermostat that help you save enerygy according to your comfort level. Through learning human comfort level, it creates a personalized energy saving plan.

Problem

Heating accounts for the largest part of energy consumption. Research shows that energy consumption can differ 200% for people living in the identical house. In addition to the differences in lifestyle, inefficient usage is also an important factor. Personal comfort and energy saving seem to be contradictory concepts. The inefficient energy usage not only increase the monthly electricity fee, but also create negative effect on our environment.

Design Process

We explored different methods of research design such as persuasive design and went through five iterations that collaboratively we created 32 design proposal with sketches and 15 interactive prototype with 80 interfaces.
