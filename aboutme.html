Exploring Users' Needs with Design and Research

RESUME

My goal and passion are to understand users' needs and to develop friendly and meaningful solutions for them. My working experience in the pediatric rehabilitation center gave me abundant opportunities to interact with people who had different needs. Through personal interaction with them and the professional observation on their performance in various contexts, I have learned to identify the core problems embedded in an intricate layout. Based on my former training in perceptual psychology and human engineering, I am also interested in UX, interaction and front-end design and continually polish these skills through self-learning.
I enjoy travelling and outdoor activities and have led a female basketball team. Through these activities, I have met and interact with people from different backgrounds with different stories, and I am enthusiastic to bring my diverse experiences into my design.
